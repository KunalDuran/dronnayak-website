The Problem

Fragmented Solutions for Autonomous Drones

Companies often struggle with ensuring reliable connectivity, deploying software updates, and monitoring drones without physically accessing the devices. This results in wasted time, higher operational costs, and inefficiencies, especially for businesses relying on autonomous drones. The lack of a streamlined system for controlling multiple drones remotely makes scaling operations difficult and prevents businesses from realizing the full potential of their drones' capabilities.

Our Solution

A Unified Connectivity Platform for Autonomous Drones

Dronnayak provides an all-in-one platform that enables real-time drone connectivity. Users can easily deploy and update applications remotely, stream telemetry data, and manage their drone fleets without needing physical access.

Key Features

Real-Time Connectivity

Stream drone telemetry data to a central server.

Remote Software Deployment

Deploy AI or other apps to drones remotely.

Remote Monitoring & Control

Monitor and control your drones remotely, reducing downtime and costs.

About Dronnayak

Dronnayak is a next-generation platform that simplifies connectivity and operational management for autonomous drone manufacturers. Founded by Kunal Duran, a DGCA-certified drone pilot with 5 years of software development experience, the platform is designed to help autonomous drone manufacturers scale their operations and focus on their core innovations.

Our team has built a Proof of Concept (POC) platform, now live, where users can add and monitor their drones and logs. We are rapidly expanding features with a well-structured development pipeline.
