About Dronnayak

Dronnayak is a next-generation platform that simplifies connectivity and operational management for autonomous drone manufacturers. Founded by Kunal Duran, a DGCA-certified drone pilot with 5 years of software development experience, the platform is designed to help manufacturers scale their operations and focus on their core innovations.

Our team has built a Proof of Concept (POC) platform, now live, where users can add and monitor their drones and logs. We are rapidly expanding features with a well-structured development pipeline.
